Library

Sathyabama University has a central library with a total area of 25,000 square feet.The library has a collection of 60,000 books.It has subscription of 334 national and international journals.and subcribes to MALIBNET(Madras Library Network), IIT Library and British Council library through which we can access the library in and around Chennai City.

Cafeteria

About US

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology (SIST), formerly Sathyabama University, is a private deemed to be university, situated at Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1987 as Sathyabama Engineering College by Jeppiaar and received its deemed to be university status in 2001.
